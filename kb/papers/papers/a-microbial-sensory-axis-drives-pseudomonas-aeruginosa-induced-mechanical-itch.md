## Abstract

Cutaneous bacterial infections frequently elicit severe pruritus, prominently featuring alloknesis, a pathological state where innocuous touch provokes intense itch. However, the peripheral mechanisms translating microbial cues into touch-evoked pruritus remain unresolved. Here, we establish an epicutaneous Pseudomonas aeruginosa infection model that robustly isolates mechanical alloknesis from spontaneous scratching.
