## Abstract

Cephalopod arms are controlled by a distributed peripheral nervous system comprising the axial nerve cord (ANC), subacetabular ganglia associated with each sucker, four longitudinal intramuscular nerve cords (INCs) embedded within the arm musculature and oblique connectives (OCs) running between INCs.
