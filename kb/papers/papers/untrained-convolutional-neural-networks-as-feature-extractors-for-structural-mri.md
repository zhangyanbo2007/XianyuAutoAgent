## Abstract

We demonstrate that features extracted from structural MRI using un-CNN, an untrained convolutional neural network, achieve predictive performance comparable to or exceeding that of state-of-the-art pretrained foundation models across three structural MRI datasets and three downstream tasks. Un-CNN extends a classical 3D CNN architecture with multi-channel inputs, a hierarchical encoder with multi-scale feature aggregation, and covariance pooling.
