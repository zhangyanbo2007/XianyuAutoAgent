原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

ArchesWeatherGen：利用机器学习实现熟练且计算高效的概率天气预报。天气预报在当今社会中扮演着至关重要的角色，从农业和物流到预测可再生能源的产出以及为极端天气事件做准备。在ERA5上以下一状态预测为目标训练的深度学习天气预报模型与数值全球环流模型相比，已显示出巨大成功。在此，我们提出了一种方法，利用确定性天气模型来设计概率天气模型，从而提高性能并降低计算成本。我们设计了一个基于流匹配（一种扩散模型的现代变体）的概率天气模型，该模型经过训练，可以将确定性天气预测投影到ERA5天气状态的分布上。我们的模型ArchesWeatherGen在所有WeatherBench主要变量上都超越了IFS ENS和NeuralGCM（NeuralGCM的位势除外）。我们的工作还旨在普及生成式机器学习模型在天气预报研究中的应用。

原始摘要

ArchesWeatherGen: Skillful and compute-­efficient probabilistic weather forecasting with machine learning Guillaume Couairon1*†, Renu Singh1†, Anastase Charantonis1, Christian Lessig2, Claire Monteleoni1,3 Weather forecasting plays a vital role in today’s society, from agriculture and logistics to predicting the output of renewable energies and preparing for extreme weather events. Deep learning weather forecasting models trained with the next state prediction objective on ERA5 have shown great success compared to numerical global circulation models. Here, we propose a methodology to leverage deterministic weather models in the design of probabilistic weather models, leading to improved performance and reduced computing costs. We design a probabilistic weather model based on flow matching, a modern variant of diffusion models, that is trained to project deterministic weather predictions to the distribution of ERA5 weather states. Our model ArchesWeatherGen surpasses IFS ENS and NeuralGCM on all WeatherBench headline variables (except for NeuralGCM’s geopotential). Our work also aims to democratize the use of generative machine learning models in weather forecasting research.
