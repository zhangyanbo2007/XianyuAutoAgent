中文对照翻译

我们提出了一个生成建模框架，用于在任意障碍物几何形状和边界条件下合成物理上可行的二维不可压缩流。现有基于扩散的流生成器要么忽略物理约束，要么施加不能保证可行性的软惩罚，要么专门用于固定几何形状，而我们的方法整合了三个互补的组件：(1) 一个作用于速度场的边界条件扩散模型；(2) 一个包含散度惩罚的物理信息训练目标；以及 (3) 一个通过几何感知的亥姆霍兹-霍奇算子强制精确不可压缩性的投影约束逆向扩散过程。我们将该方法推导为散度自由向量场流形上约束朗之万采样的离散近似，从而建立了现代扩散模型与不可压缩流空间中几何约束强制执行之间的联系。在解析纳维-斯托克斯数据和障碍物边界流配置上的实验表明，相对于无约束、仅投影和仅惩罚的基线，我们的方法在散度、谱精度、涡度统计和边界一致性方面均有显著改善。我们的公式统一了扩散模型中的软硬物理结构，并为机器人学、图形学和科学计算领域中不可压缩场的生成建模奠定了基础。

原始摘要

We present a generative modeling framework for synthesizing physically feasible two-dimensional incompressible flows under arbitrary obstacle geometries and boundary conditions. Whereas existing diffusion-based flow generators either ignore physical constraints, impose soft penalties that do not guarantee feasibility, or specialize to fixed geometries, our approach integrates three complementary components: (1) a boundary-conditioned diffusion model operating on velocity fields; (2) a physics-informed training objective incorporating a divergence penalty; and (3) a projection-constrained reverse diffusion process that enforces exact incompressibility through a geometry-aware Helmholtz–Hodge operator. We derive the method as a discrete approximation to constrained Langevin sampling on the manifold of divergence-free vector fields, providing a connection between modern diffusion models and geometric constraint enforcement in incompressible flow spaces. Experiments on analytic Navier–Stokes data and obstacle-bounded flow configurations demonstrate significantly improved divergence, spectral accuracy, vorticity statistics, and boundary consistency relative to unconstrained, projectiononly, and penalty-only baselines. Our formulation unifies soft and hard physical structure within diffusion models and provides a foundation for generative modeling of incompressible fields in robotics, graphics, and scientific computing.
