尽管AI智能体在推理和工具使用方面展现出卓越能力，但它们本质上仍然是被动的：它们仅在接收到明确的用户提示后才计算响应。这种范式忽略了一个关键机会：交互之间的空闲时间大部分被浪费了，导致智能体无法为未来的用户需求做准备。为了弥补这一空白，我们引入了ProAct，一个主动式智能体架构，它利用空闲时间计算来预测并满足可能即将到来的用户需求。通过分析不断演变的对话历史和持久记忆，ProAct预测即将到来的需求并迭代地获取信息，使智能体能够在用户发起查询之前解决知识空白并准备证据。为了严格评估主动能力，我们还引入了ProActEval，一个包含40个领域200个场景的综合基准，具有可预测的需求链和多样化的用户认知画像。实验结果表明，与被动基线相比，ProAct具有显著优势。ProAct通过将所需轮次减少14.8%来加速任务完成，将用户工作量减少11.7%，并在ProActEval上将幻觉率降低28.1%。此外，MemBench评估证实ProAct实现了最先进的反射准确性，强调了其持续和稳健的性能。# 联络方式：Weiwen Liu (wwliu@sjtu.edu.cn) § 代码：https://github.com/AgentACE-AI/ProAct

原始摘要

While AI agents demonstrate remarkable capabilities in reasoning and tool use, they remain fundamentally reactive: they compute responses only after explicit user prompts. This paradigm ignores a critical opportunity: the idle time between interactions is largely wasted, leaving agents unable to prepare for future user needs. To bridge this gap, we introduce ProAct, a proactive agent architecture that leverages idle-time compute to anticipate and fulfill likely upcoming user needs. By analyzing evolving dialogue history together with persistent memory, ProAct predicts upcoming needs and iteratively acquires information, allowing the agent to resolve knowledge gaps and prepare evidence before the user initiates a query. To rigorously evaluate proactive capabilities, we also introduce ProActEval, a comprehensive benchmark comprising 200 scenarios across 40 domains, featuring predictable need chains and diverse user cognitive profiles. Empirical results demonstrate significant advantages over reactive baselines. ProAct accelerates task completion by reducing required turns by 14.8%, decreases user effort by 11.7%, and cuts hallucination rates by 28.1% on ProActEval. Furthermore, MemBench evaluations confirm that ProAct achieves state-of-the-art reflective accuracy, underscoring its sustained and robust performance. # Contact: Weiwen Liu (wwliu@sjtu.edu.cn) § Code: https://github.com/AgentACE-AI/ProAct
