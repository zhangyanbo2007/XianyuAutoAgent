中文对照翻译

我们引入FinWorkBench（又称F INCH），用于评估代理在真实世界、企业级财务和会计工作流程中的表现——这些工作流程交织着数据录入、结构化、格式化、网络搜索、跨文件检索、计算、建模、验证、翻译、可视化和报告。F INCH的数据来源于安然（15,000个文件和500,000封电子邮件）以及各种金融机构的真实企业工作空间，涵盖2000年至2025年期间，保留了多模态工件（如表格和图表）在实际环境中的混乱性，涉及预算、交易和资产管理等多个领域。我们提出了一种工作流程构建过程，该过程结合了LLM辅助的真实企业环境工作流程挖掘和专家标注：（1）LLM辅助、专家验证的从真实电子邮件线程和电子表格文件版本历史中推导工作流程，以及（2）对工作流程进行细致标注，这需要超过700小时的专家投入。这产生了172个复合工作流程，包含384项任务，涉及1,710个电子表格，共计2700万个单元格，以及PDF和其他工件，捕捉了企业工作固有的混乱性、长周期性、知识密集性和协作性。我们对前沿AI系统进行了人工和自动化评估，包括GPT 5.1、Claude Sonnet/Opus 4.5、Gemini 3 Pro、Grok 4和Qwen 3 Max。GPT 5.1 Pro平均每个工作流程花费16.8分钟，但仅通过了38.4%的工作流程。全面的案例研究进一步揭示了真实世界企业工作流程对AI代理构成的挑战。数据集：https://huggingface.co/FinWorkBench ∗

原始摘要

We introduce FinWorkBench (a.k.a. F INCH) for evaluating agents on real-world, enterprise-grade finance and accounting workflows—interleaving data entry, structuring, formatting, web search, cross-file retrieval, calculation, modeling, validation, translation, visualization, and reporting. F INCH is sourced from authentic enterprise workspaces from Enron (15,000 files and 500,000 emails) and across various financial institutions, covering the period 2000–2025, preserving the in-the-wild messiness of multimodal artifacts such as tables and charts, across diverse domains including budgeting, trading, and asset management. We propose a workflow construction process that combines LLM-assisted mining of workflows from authentic enterprise environments with expert annotation: (1) LLMassisted, expert-verified derivation of workflows from real-world email threads and version histories of spreadsheet files, and (2) meticulous annotation for workflows, requiring over 700 hours of expert effort. This yields 172 composite workflows with 384 tasks, involving 1,710 spreadsheets with 27 million cells, along with PDFs and other artifacts, capturing the intrinsically messy, long-horizon, knowledgeintensive, and collaborative nature of enterprise work. We conduct both human and automated evaluations of frontier AI systems, including GPT 5.1, Claude Sonnet/Opus 4.5, Gemini 3 Pro, Grok 4, and Qwen 3 Max. GPT 5.1 Pro spends an average of 16.8 minutes per workflow yet passes only 38.4% of workflows. Comprehensive case studies further surface the challenges that real-world enterprise workflows pose for AI agents. Dataset: https://huggingface.co/FinWorkBench ∗
