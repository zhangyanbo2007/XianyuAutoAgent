原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

视觉语言模型 (VLMs) 通过提示实现对各种视觉任务的统一建模，并展现出在语义理解方面的良好表现。然而，3D 理解仍然很大程度上依赖于具有复杂任务特定设计的专家视觉模型。本文的核心论点是 VLMs 本质上是原生 3D 学习者。我们的深入大规模研究表明，1)焦距统一，2)基于文本的像素参考，以及 3)数据混合与缩放，都是有效 3D 学习所必需的。模型架构变化、大型模型、大量的增强数据以及包括回归形式在内的复杂损失，许多这些都是专家视觉模型的基础，实际上并非必要条件。因此，我们提出 VLM3，一种可扩展的方法，具有最简单的设计，能够使标准 VLMs 掌握各种 3D 任务。VLM3 不仅大幅提高了 VLM 的深度估计准确率（从 0.84 提升到 0.9），还使像素对应、相机姿态估计和对象级别的 3D 理解等多种 3D 任务得以实现，同时保持标准架构和基于文本的训练，并匹配专家视觉模型精度。我们相信 VLM3 为简单且可扩展的 3D 学习开辟了一条新途径。

原始摘要

Vision Language Models (VLMs) enable a unified model to solve various vision tasks through prompting. They have shown promising performance in semantic understanding. However, 3D understanding still largely relies on expert vision models with complex task-specific designs. The key argument this work wants to make is that VLMs are native 3D learners. Our in-depth large scale study shows that 1) focal length unification, 2) text-based pixel reference and 3) data mixture and scaling, are all you need for effective 3D learning. Model architecture changes, large models, heavy data augmentations, and complex losses including the regression formulation, many of which form the foundation of expert vision models, are actually not necessary conditions. As a result, we propose VLM3 , a scalable method with the simplest design that enables standard VLMs to master diverse 3D tasks. VLM3 not only advances the VLM depth estimation accuracy by a large margin (0.84 → 0.9), but also enables diverse 3D tasks such as pixel correspondence, camera pose estimation and object-level 3D understanding, matching expert vision model accuracy while maintaining standard architectures and text-based training. We believe VLM3 opens up a new paradigm for simple and scalable 3D learning. Date: June 1, 2026 Correspondence: Zhipeng Cai czptc2h@gmail.com Code: https://github.com/facebookresearch/VLM3
