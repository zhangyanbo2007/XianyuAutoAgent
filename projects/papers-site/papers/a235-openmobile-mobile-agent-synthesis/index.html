中文对照翻译

由视觉-语言模型驱动的移动智能体在自动化移动任务方面展现出令人印象深刻的能力，近期领先的模型取得了显著的性能飞跃，例如在AndroidWorld上成功率接近70%。然而，这些系统对其训练数据保密，并且对其任务和轨迹合成方法保持不透明。我们提出了OpenMobile，一个开源框架，用于合成高质量的任务指令和智能体轨迹，包含两个关键组件：(1) 第一个是一个可扩展的任务合成管道，它通过探索构建全局环境记忆，然后利用它生成多样化且有根据的指令。(2) 一个用于轨迹展开的策略切换策略。通过在学习者模型和专家模型之间交替，它捕获了标准模仿学习中经常缺失的基本错误恢复数据。在我们数据上训练的智能体在三个动态移动智能体基准测试中取得了有竞争力的结果：值得注意的是，我们微调的Qwen2.5-VL和Qwen3-VL在AndroidWorld上分别达到了51.7%和64.7%，远远超过了现有的开放数据方法。此外，我们对合成指令和基准测试集之间的重叠进行了透明分析，并验证了性能提升源于广泛的功能覆盖，而非基准过拟合。我们在OpenMobile发布了数据和代码，以弥合数据鸿沟并促进更广泛的移动智能体研究。

原始摘要

Mobile agents powered by vision-language models have demonstrated impressive capabilities in automating mobile tasks, with recent leading models achieving a marked performance leap, e.g., nearly 70% success on AndroidWorld. However, these systems keep their training data closed and remain opaque about their task and trajectory synthesis recipes. We present OpenMobile, an open-source framework that synthesizes high-quality task instructions and agent trajectories, with two key components: (1) The first is a scalable task synthesis pipeline that constructs a global environment memory from exploration, then leverages it to generate diverse and grounded instructions. and (2) a policy-switching strategy for trajectory rollout. By alternating between learner and expert models, it captures essential errorrecovery data often missing in standard imitation learning. Agents trained on our data achieve competitive results across three dynamic mobile agent benchmarks: notably, our fine-tuned Qwen2.5-VL and Qwen3-VL reach 51.7% and 64.7% on AndroidWorld, far surpassing existing open-data approaches. Furthermore, we conduct transparent analyses on the overlap between our synthetic instructions and benchmark test sets, and verify that performance gains stem from broad functionality coverage rather than benchmark overfitting.
