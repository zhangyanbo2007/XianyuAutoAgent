中文对照翻译

网络代理——代表用户在网络上导航和执行任务的自主系统——有潜力改变人们与数字世界的互动方式。然而，当今最强大的网络代理依赖于专有模型，其训练数据和方法未公开，这限制了科学理解、可复现性和社区驱动的进步。我们认为开放网络的代理应该以开放的方式构建。为此，我们引入了 (1) MolmoWebMix，一个大型且多样化的浏览器任务演示和网络GUI感知数据混合集，以及 (2) MolmoWeb，一个完全开放的多模态网络代理家族。具体来说，MolmoWebMix结合了来自多个互补生成管道的超过10万条合成任务轨迹，以及3万多条人类演示、原子级网络技能轨迹和GUI感知数据，包括指代表达接地和屏幕截图问答。MolmoWeb代理作为指令条件下的视觉语言动作策略运行：给定任务指令和网页屏幕截图，它们预测下一个浏览器动作，无需访问HTML、可访问性树或专用API。MolmoWeb代理提供4B和8B两种规模，在WebVoyager、Online-Mind2Web和DeepShop等浏览器使用基准测试中，MolmoWeb代理取得了最先进的结果，超越了Fara-7B、UI-Tars-1.5-7B和Holo1-7B等同等规模的仅开放权重模型。MolmoWeb-8B还超越了基于GPT-4o等更大规模的封闭前沿模型构建的标记集（SoM）代理。我们通过使用最佳N选择的并行运行进行测试时扩展，进一步展示了持续的收益，在WebVoyager和Online-Mind2Web上实现了94.7%和60.5%的pass@4（相比之下，pass@1分别为78.2%和35.3%）。

原始摘要

Web agents—autonomous systems that navigate and execute tasks on the web on behalf of users—have the potential to transform how people interact with the digital world. However, the most capable web agents today rely on proprietary models with undisclosed training data and recipes, limiting scientific understanding, reproducibility, and community-driven progress. We believe agents for the open web should be built in the open. To this end, we introduce (1) MolmoWebMix, a large and diverse mixture of browser task demonstrations and web-GUI perception data and (2) MolmoWeb a family of fully open multimodal web agents. Specifically, MolmoWebMix combines over 100K synthetic task trajectories from multiple complementary generation pipelines with 30K+ human demonstrations, atomic web-skill trajectories, and GUI perception data, including referring expression grounding and screenshot question answering. MolmoWeb agents operate as instruction-conditioned visual-language action policies: given a task instruction and a webpage screenshot, they predict the next browser action, requiring no access to HTML, accessibility trees, or specialized APIs. Available in 4B and 8B size, on browser-use benchmarks like WebVoyager, Online-Mind2Web, and DeepShop, MolmoWeb agents achieve state-of-the-art results outperforming similar scale open-weight-only models such as Fara-7B, UI-Tars-1.5-7B, and Holo1-7B. MolmoWeb-8B also surpasses set-of-marks (SoM) agents built on much larger closed frontier models like GPT-4o. We further demonstrate consistent gains through test-time scaling via parallel rollouts with best-of-N selection, achieving 94.7% and 60.5% pass@4 (compared to 78.2% and 35.3% pass@1)on WebVoyager and Online-Mind2Web
