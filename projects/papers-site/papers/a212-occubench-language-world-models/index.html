原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

AI智能体有望在数百个职业领域（从急诊科分诊到核反应堆安全监控再到海关进口处理）执行专业工作，然而现有基准只能在少数存在公共环境的领域评估智能体。我们引入了O CCU B ENCH，一个涵盖10个行业类别和65个专业领域中100个真实世界专业任务场景的基准，该基准通过语言世界模型（LWMs）实现，LWMs通过LLM驱动的工具响应生成来模拟领域特定环境。我们的多智能体合成管道自动生成具有保证可解性、校准难度和文档基础多样性的评估实例。O CCU B ENCH从两个互补维度评估智能体：跨专业领域的任务完成度以及在受控故障注入（显式错误、隐式数据降级和混合故障）下的环境鲁棒性。我们评估了8个模型家族的15个前沿模型，发现：(1) 没有单一模型能主导所有行业，因为每个模型都有独特的职业能力概况；(2) 隐式故障（截断数据、缺失字段）比显式错误（超时、500s）和混合故障更难，因为它们缺乏明显的错误信号，需要智能体独立检测数据降级；(3) 更大的模型、更新的代际和更高的推理努力持续提升性能。GPT-5.2从最小推理努力到最大推理努力提升了27.5分；(4) 强大的智能体不一定是强大的环境模拟器。模拟器质量对于基于LWM的评估可靠性至关重要。O CCU B ENCH首次对AI智能体在专业职业任务上进行了系统的跨行业评估。

原始摘要

AI agents are expected to perform professional work across hundreds of occupational domains (from emergency department triage to nuclear reactor safety monitoring to customs import processing), yet existing benchmarks can only evaluate agents in the few domains where public environments exist. We introduce O CCU B ENCH, a benchmark covering 100 real-world professional task scenarios across 10 industry categories and 65 specialized domains, enabled by Language World Models (LWMs) that simulate domainspecific environments through LLM-driven tool response generation. Our multi-agent synthesis pipeline automatically produces evaluation instances with guaranteed solvability, calibrated difficulty, and document-grounded diversity. O CCU B ENCH evaluates agents along two complementary dimensions: task completion across professional domains and environmental robustness under controlled fault injection (explicit errors, implicit data degradation, and mixed faults). We evaluate 15 frontier models across 8 model families and find that: (1) no single model dominates all industries, as each has a distinct occupational capability profile; (2) implicit faults (truncated data, missing fields) are harder than both explicit errors (timeouts, 500s) and mixed faults, because they lack overt error signals and require the agent to independently detect data degradation; (3) larger models, newer generations, and higher reasoning effort consistently improve performance. GPT-5.2 improves by 27.5 points from minimal to maximum reasoning effort; and (4) strong agents are not necessarily strong environment simulators. Simulator quality is critical for LWM-based evaluation reliability. O CCU B ENCH provides the first systematic cross-industry evaluation of AI agents on professional occupational tasks.
