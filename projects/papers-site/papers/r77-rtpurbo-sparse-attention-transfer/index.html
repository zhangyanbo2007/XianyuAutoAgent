中文对照翻译

大型语言模型（LLMs）中的长上下文推理受限于全注意力的二次方成本。现有的高效替代方案通常依赖于原生稀疏训练或启发式令牌逐出，在效率、训练成本和准确性之间造成了不理想的权衡。在这项工作中，我们表明全注意力LLMs本身就是稀疏的，并且只需少量适应即可转换为高度稀疏模型。我们的方法基于三个观察结果：（1）只有一小部分注意力头真正需要完整的长上下文处理；（2）长距离检索主要由低维子空间控制，允许使用16维索引器高效检索相关令牌；（3）有用令牌预算强烈依赖于查询，使得动态top-p选择比固定top-k稀疏化更合适。基于这些见解，我们提出了RTPurbo，它仅为检索头保留完整的KV缓存，并引入了一个轻量级令牌索引器用于稀疏注意力。通过利用模型的内在稀疏性，RTPurbo仅用数百个训练步骤就实现了稀疏化。在长上下文基准测试和推理任务上的实验表明，RTPurbo保持了近乎无损的准确性，同时提供了显著的效率提升，包括在1M上下文下预填充速度提升高达9.36倍，解码速度提升约2.01倍。这些结果表明，强大的稀疏推理可以从标准的全注意力训练中获得，而无需昂贵的原生稀疏预训练。E-mail: yankezhou@smail.nju.edu.cn wanghaisheng.whs@alibaba-inc.com y.yao@nju.edu.cn

原始摘要

Long-context inference in large language models is bottlenecked by the quadratic cost of full attention. Existing efficient alternatives often rely either on native sparse training or on heuristic token eviction, creating an undesirable trade-off among efficiency, training cost, and accuracy. In this work, we show that full-attention LLMs are already intrinsically sparse and can be transformed into highly sparse models with only minimal adaptation. Our approach is built on three observations: (1) only a small subset of attention heads truly requires full long-context processing; (2) long-range retrieval is governed primarily by a low-dimensional subspace, allowing relevant tokens to be retrieved efficiently with a 16-dimensional indexer; and (3) the useful token budget is strongly query-dependent, making dynamic top-p selection more suitable than fixed top-k sparsification. Based on these insights, we propose RTPurbo, which retains the full KV cache only for retrieval heads and introduces a lightweight token indexer for sparse attention. By exploiting the model’s intrinsic sparsity, RTPurbo achieves sparsification with only a few hundred training steps. Experiments on long-context benchmarks and reasoning tasks show that RTPurbo preserves near-lossless accuracy while delivering substantial efficiency gains, including up to a 9.36× prefill speedup at 1M context and about a 2.01× decode speedup. These results suggest that strong sparse inference can be obtained from standard full-attention training without expensive native sparse pretraining.
