原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

人工智能的快速发展引发了对其可信赖性的担忧，尤其是在可解释性和鲁棒性方面。随机森林等基于树的模型在表格数据的可解释性和准确性方面表现出色，但由于数据局部性差和数据依赖性高，对其进行扩展仍然计算成本高昂。以前利用模拟内容可寻址存储器 (CAM) 加速这些模型的尝试一直面临困难，因为难以实现的尖锐决策边界极易受器件变异的影响，导致硬件性能不佳并易受对抗性攻击。在本文中，我们提出了一种采用基于MoS2闪存的模拟CAM并具有固有软边界的软硬件协同设计方法，从而能够使用软基于树的模型进行高效推理。我们制造的模拟CAM阵列在威斯康星州诊断乳腺癌数据集上实现了96%的准确率，并且我们实验校准的模型在10%器件阈值变异下，在MNIST数据集上仅显示0.6%的准确率下降，而传统决策树的下降幅度为45.3%。

原始摘要

The rapid advancement of artificial intelligence has raised concerns regarding its trustworthiness, especially in terms of interpretability and robustness. Tree-based models such as Random Forest excel in interpretability and accuracy for tabular data, but scaling them remains computationally expensive due to poor data locality and high data dependence. Previous efforts to accelerate these models with analog content-addressable memory (CAM) have struggled because difficult-to-implement sharp decision boundaries are highly susceptible to device variations, leading to poor hardware performance and vulnerability to adversarial attacks. Here, we present a hardware-software co-design approach using MoS 2 flash-based analog CAM with inherent soft boundaries, enabling efficient inference with soft tree-based models. Our fabricated analog CAM arrays achieve 96% accuracy on the Wisconsin Diagnostic Breast Cancer dataset, and our experimentally calibrated model shows only a 0.6% accuracy drop on MNIST under 10% device threshold variation, compared with 45.3% for traditional decision trees.
