原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

标题：预训练视频模型作为城市风流的可微分物理模拟器。设计能够提供行人风舒适度和安全性的城市空间需要时间分辨的计算流体动力学（CFD）模拟，但其当前的计算成本使得广泛的设计探索不切实际。我们引入了WinDiNet（风扩散网络），这是一个预训练的视频扩散模型，被重新用作此任务的快速、可微分替代模型。我们从LTX-Video（一个20亿参数的潜在视频Transformer）开始，在10,000个程序生成建筑布局的2D不可压缩CFD模拟上进行微调。对训练方案、条件机制和VAE适应策略（包括物理信息解码器损失）的系统研究，确定了一种性能优于专用神经偏微分方程求解器的配置。所得模型在不到一秒的时间内生成完整的112帧展开。由于该替代模型是端到端可微分的，它也可用作基于梯度的逆向优化的物理模拟器：给定一个城市占地布局，我们通过反向传播直接优化建筑物位置，以提高风安全性和行人风舒适度。在单入口和多入口布局上的实验表明，即使在具有挑战性的多目标配置下，优化器也能发现有效的布局，所有改进均通过真实CFD模拟得到证实。

原始摘要

Designing urban spaces that provide pedestrian wind comfort and safety requires time-resolved Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations, but their current computational cost makes extensive design exploration impractical. We introduce WinDiNet (Wind Diffusion Network), a pretrained video diffusion model that is repurposed as a fast, differentiable surrogate for this task. Starting from LTX-Video, a 2B-parameter latent video transformer, we fine-tune on 10,000 2D incompressible CFD simulations over procedurally generated building layouts. A systematic study of training regimes, conditioning mechanisms, and VAE adaptation strategies, including a physics-informed decoder loss, identifies a configuration that outperforms purpose-built neural PDE solvers. The resulting model generates full 112-frame rollouts in under a second. As the surrogate is end-to-end differentiable, it doubles as a physics simulator for gradient-based inverse optimization: given an urban footprint layout, we optimize building positions directly through backpropagation to improve wind safety as well as pedestrian wind comfort. Experiments on singleand multi-inlet layouts show that the optimizer discovers effective layouts even under challenging multi-objective configurations, with all improvements confirmed by ground-truth CFD simulations.
