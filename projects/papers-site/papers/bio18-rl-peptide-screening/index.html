原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

生物活性肽，如抗癌肽 (ACPs)，因其对肿瘤的有效性和选择性以及对健康人体细胞的最小毒性，为小分子药物提供了一种有前景的治疗替代方案。然而，它们的合理发现需要利用计算量大的计算机模拟工具在广阔的化学空间中进行探索。在本文中，我们提出了一种计算方法，利用强化学习和后验采样，能够经济高效地探索大型肽库。所开发方法的实际应用成功识别出具有治疗潜力的溶膜肽。所开发的计算方法与穷举式库筛选相比，将搜索空间减少了90%以上，并实现了数据集探索与利用之间的有效平衡。我们通过筛选一个包含3600万个从蛋白质数据库中整理得到的结构解析螺旋肽的聚焦库，证明了该方法的可扩展性。在体外试验中进行筛选时，选定的前100个候选物中有15个对乳腺癌细胞（包括耐药性三阴性乳腺癌）表现出细胞毒性活性，其中三种先导化合物进一步被证实对健康人体细胞无毒。这项研究强调了使用深度强化学习加速生物活性肽发现的潜力，为开发新型肽基癌症疗法提供了一条有前景的途径。

原始摘要

Bioactive peptides such as anticancer peptides (ACPs) offer a promising therapeutic alternative to small molecules due to their efficiency and selectivity against tumors and minimal toxicity towards healthy human cells. However, their rational discovery requires navigating a vast chemical space using computationally demanding in silico tools. Herein, we present a computational method enabling cost-efficient exploration of large peptide libraries using reinforcement learning and posterior sampling. Practical application of the developed approach results in identification of membranolytic peptides with therapeutic potential. The developed computational method reduces the search space by over 90% compared to exhaustive library screening and enables effective balancing between dataset’s exploration and exploitation. We demonstrate the scalability of this method by screening a focused library of 36 million structurally resolved helical peptides curated from the Protein Data Bank. When screened in in vitro assays, 15 of the top 100 selected candidates exhibit cytotoxic activity against breast cancer cells including drug resistant triple-negative breast cancer, with the three lead compounds further characterizing as non-toxic towards healthy human cells. This study highlights the potential of using deep reinforcement learning to expedite bioactive peptide discovery, offering a promising path for developing new peptide-based cancer therapies.
