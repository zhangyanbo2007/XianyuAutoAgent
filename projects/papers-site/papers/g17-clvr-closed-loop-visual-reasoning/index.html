原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

尽管取得了快速发展，当前的文本到图像 (T2I) 模型主要依赖于单步生成范式，这难以处理复杂语义，并且面临参数扩展的收益递减。虽然最近的多步推理方法显示出前景，但它们受到缺乏验证的无根据规划幻觉、单一的后验反思、长上下文优化不稳定性以及过高的推理延迟的阻碍。为了克服这些瓶颈，我们提出了闭环视觉推理 (CLVR) 框架，这是一个将视觉-语言逻辑规划与像素级扩散生成深度耦合的综合系统。CLVR引入了一个带有步级视觉验证的自动化数据引擎，以合成可靠的推理轨迹，并提出了代理提示强化学习 (PPRL) 来通过将交错的多模态历史提炼成显式奖励信号以实现准确的因果归因，从而解决长上下文优化不稳定性。此外，为了缓解迭代去噪引起的严重延迟瓶颈，我们提出了∆-空间权重合并 (DSWM)，这是一种理论基础的方法，它将对齐权重与现成的蒸馏先验融合，将每步推理成本降低到仅4个NFE，而无需昂贵的再蒸馏。大量实验表明，CLVR在多个基准测试中优于现有开源基线，并接近专有商业模型的性能，从而释放了复杂视觉生成的通用测试时扩展能力。

原始摘要

Despite rapid advancements, current text-to-image (T2I) models predominantly rely on a single-step generation paradigm, which struggles with complex semantics and faces diminishing returns from parameter scaling. While recent multi-step reasoning approaches show promise, they are hindered by ungrounded planning hallucinations lacking verification, monolithic post-hoc reflection, long-context optimization instabilities, and prohibitive inference latency. To overcome these bottlenecks, we propose the Closed-Loop Visual Reasoning (CLVR) framework, a comprehensive system that deeply couples visual-language logical planning with pixel-level diffusion generation. CLVR introduces an automated data engine Preprint. with step-level visual verification to synthesize reliable reasoning trajectories, and proposes Proxy Prompt Reinforcement Learning (PPRL) to resolve longcontext optimization instabilities by distilling interleaved multimodal histories into explicit reward signals for accurate causal attribution. Furthermore, to mitigate the severe latency bottleneck caused by iterative denoising, we propose ∆-Space Weight Merge (DSWM), a theoretically grounded method that fuses alignment weights with off-the-shelf distillation priors, reducing the per-step inference cost to just 4 NFEs without requiring expensive re-distillation. Extensive experiments demonstrate that CLVR outperforms existing open-source baselines across multiple benchmarks and approaches the performance of proprietary commercial models, unlocking general test-time scaling capabilities for complex visual generation.
