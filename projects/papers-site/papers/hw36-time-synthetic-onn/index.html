原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

光神经网络（ONN）提供了超快和节能的人工智能，但其有效深度仍然受到根本限制，因为核心线性变换绝大多数是被动的，并且累积损耗会迅速降低信噪比。原则上，将光增益引入空间光子网格可以抵消这种衰减，但由于不可避免的反馈路径和寄生反射，这种放大是出了名的不稳定。在此，我们通过将可编程增益集成到时间合成光神经网络中来克服这一长期存在的限制，其中计算通过严格的前向时间演化而非空间干涉层展开。这种因果拓扑抑制了触发增益引起的失稳的反向通道，从而实现了稳定的损耗补偿并显著扩展了网络的可用深度。数值分析和原位实验证明了在图像分类任务上的稳健性能，将增益辅助时间合成光神经网络确立为一种稳定、可扩展和可编程的途径，以实现超越主要无源架构限制的深度光子智能。

原始摘要

Optical neural networks (ONNs) offer ultrafast and energy-efficient artificial intelligence, yet their effective depth remains fundamentally limited because the core linear transformations are overwhelmingly passive, and cumulative loss rapidly degrades the signal-to-noise ratio. Introducing optical gain into spatial photonic meshes could, in principle, counteract this decay, but such amplification is notoriously unstable owing to unavoidable feedback paths and parasitic reflections. Here, we overcome this long-standing limitation by integrating programmable gain into a time-synthetic ONN, where computation unfolds through strictly forward temporal evolution rather than spatial interferometric layers. This causal topology suppresses the backward channels that trigger gain-induced instabilities, enabling stable loss compensation and substantially extending the network’s usable depth. Numerical analysis and in-situ experiments demonstrate robust performance on image-classification tasks, establishing gain-assisted time-synthetic ONNs as a stable, scalable, and programmable pathway toward deep photonic intelligence beyond the limitations of predominantly passive architectures.
