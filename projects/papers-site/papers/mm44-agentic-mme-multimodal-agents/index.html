中文对照翻译

多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）正在从被动观察者迅速演变为主动智能体。这些智能体越来越多地通过视觉扩展（Visual Expansion）来解决问题，主动调用视觉工具来转换图像。此外，由于现实世界任务通常需要超出视觉内容的信息，现代系统将这些视觉操作与通过开放网络搜索进行的知识扩展（Knowledge Expansion）相结合。然而，现有评估在三个关键方面存在不足。(i) 工具集成缺乏灵活性和全面性，无法支持异构工具接口。(ii) 分别测试图像工具使用和网络搜索，未探索它们的协同作用。(iii) 主要通过最终答案的正确性进行评估。因此，它们无法验证工具是否实际被调用、正确应用或高效使用。为了回答智能体能力真正为多模态智能带来了什么，我们引入了Agentic-MME，一个用于多模态智能体能力的流程验证基准。Agentic-MME包含418个跨6个领域和3个难度级别的真实世界任务，旨在评估能力协同作用，其特点是超过2,000个分步检查点，每个任务平均需要超过10个人工小时进行手动标注。每个任务都配有(i) 一个支持沙盒代码执行和结构化工具API的统一评估框架，以及(ii) 一个沿S轴和V轴双轴标注了分步检查点的人类参考轨迹。为了实现真正的流程级验证，我们通过审计细粒度的中间状态来超越最终答案。我们还通过相对于人类轨迹的“过度思考”指标来量化效率。实验结果表明，最佳模型Gemini3-pro的总体准确率为56.3%，在Level-3任务上该分数显著下降至23.0%，突显了真实世界多模态智能体问题解决的难度。关键词：智能体MLLMs，基准，深度研究级别1：单一决定性视觉操作。问题：图片中有一个品牌，它在第二次世界大战结束后成立于法国。请找到这个品牌。它的右边是另一个历史悠久的品牌。在这个第二个品牌成立的那一年，有一个州加入了美国。到2025年，这个州将法定最低工资提高到每小时多少美元？

原始摘要

Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) are rapidly evolving from passive observers into active agents. These agents increasingly solve problems through Visual Expansion, actively invoking visual tools to transform images. Furthermore, as real-world tasks often require information beyond visual content, modern systems combine these visual operations with Knowledge Expansion via open-web search. However, existing evaluations fall short in three critical aspects. (i) Lack flexibility and comprehensiveness in tool integration that supports heterogeneous tool interfaces. (ii) Test image tool use and web search separately, leaving their synergy unexplored. (iii) Evaluate primarily by the correctness of the final answer. Consequently, they cannot verify whether the tools were actually invoked, applied correctly, or used efficiently. To answer what agentic capability truly brings to multimodal intelligence, we introduce Agentic-MME, a process-verified benchmark for Multimodal Agentic Capabilities. Agentic-MME contains 418 real-world tasks across 6 domains and 3 difficulty levels designed to evaluate capability synergy, featuring over 2,000 stepwise checkpoints that average more than 10 person-hours of manual annotation per task. Each task is paired with (i) a unified evaluation framework that supports both sandboxed code execution and structured tool APIs, and (ii) a human reference trajectory annotated with stepwise checkpoints along dual-axis: S-axis and V-axis. To enable true process-level verification, we move beyond final answers by auditing fine-grained intermediate states. We also quantify efficiency via an overthinking metric relative to human trajectories. Experimental results show that the best model Gemini3-pro achieves an overall accuracy of 56.3% and this score falls significantly to 23.0% on Level-3 tasks, underscoring the difficulty of real-world multimodal agentic problem solving.Keywords: Agentic MLLMs, Benchmark, Deep-Research Level 1: single decisive visual operation. Question: In the picture, there is a brand that was founded in France after the end of World War II. Please locate this brand. To its right is another brand with a long history. In the year this second brand was founded, a state joined the United States. To how many dollars per hour did this state raise its legal minimum wage in 2025?
