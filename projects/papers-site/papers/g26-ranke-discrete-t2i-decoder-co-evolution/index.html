原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

离散自回归（AR）文本到图像（T2I）模型将VQ分词器与AR策略配对，而当前的后训练流程仅优化策略，同时保持VQ解码器冻结。最近以REPAE为代表的扩散T2I工作表明，VAE本身构成了关键的对齐瓶颈，但对于离散AR模型尚无类似的研究。我们表明，仅优化策略会导致潜在协变量偏移：随着策略的演变，生成的token分布偏离了解码器训练所依据的真实分布，导致奖励分数提高而解码图像质量下降。为了解决这种不匹配，我们提出了RankE，这是第一个用于离散T2I生成的端到端后训练框架。RankE不是针对固定解码器优化策略，而是通过交替优化共同演化这两个组件：每个模块最大化一个基于排名的对齐目标，同时受到一个适合其参数空间的稳定性保持锚点的正则化。这种共同演化打破了困扰冻结解码器方法的保真度-对齐权衡：在LlamaGen-XL (775M)上，标准RL提高了CLIP但降低了FID，而RankE同时提高了两者（在MSCOCO 30K上FID 15.21，CLIP 33.76）。在Janus-Pro (1B)上的一致增益证实，解码器共同演化能够可靠地将奖励优化转化为像素空间质量的提升。

原始摘要

Discrete autoregressive (AR) text-to-image (T2I) models pair a VQ tokenizer with an AR policy, and current post-training pipelines optimize only the policy while keeping the VQ decoder frozen. Recent diffusion T2I work, exemplified by REPAE, has shown that the VAE itself constitutes a key alignment bottleneck, yet no analogous investigation exists for discrete AR models. We show that policy-only optimization induces Latent Covariate Shift: as the policy evolves, the resulting token distribution diverges from the ground-truth distribution on which the decoder was trained, such that reward scores improve while decoded image quality degrades. To address this mismatch, we propose RankE, the first end-to-end post-training framework for discrete T2I generation. Rather than optimizing the policy against a fixed decoder, RankE co-evolves both components through alternating optimization: each module maximizes a ranking-based alignment objective while being regularized by a stability-preserving anchor suited to its parameter space. This co-evolution breaks the fidelity–alignment trade-off that plagues frozen-decoder approaches: on LlamaGen-XL (775M), standard RL improves CLIP but degrades FID, whereas RankE improves both simultaneously (FID 15.21, CLIP 33.76 on MSCOCO 30K). Consistent gains on Janus-Pro (1B) confirm that decoder co-evolution reliably converts reward optimization into pixel-space quality improvements.
