原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

我们通过在快速沃尔什-哈达玛域中量化角度来压缩KV缓存条目，其中随机的±1对角旋转使得连续的元素对在单位圆上均匀分布。我们将这种角度量化器扩展为逐层早期增强：在每一层独立配置K和V码本大小，并对模型特定的关键层子集提供更高的精度。在七个模型（1B到7B参数）中，逐层早期增强在四个模型上实现了无损压缩（∆PPL ≤ 0），在另外两个模型上实现了近乎无损的质量（∆PPL ≤ 0.002），每个元素使用3.28到3.67个角度比特。添加范数量化并采用非对称K/V比特分配（8比特线性K范数，4比特对数空间V范数）后，在Mistral-7B上实现了6.56总比特的端到端速率，且∆PPL仅为+0.0014，无需任何校准。层组敏感性分析揭示，关键层、瓶颈类型（K主导型 vs V主导型），甚至存在精度增加反而降低质量的负迁移层，都具有模型特异性，这为在新架构上配置量化器提供了可操作的规则。

原始摘要

We compress KV cache entries by quantizing angles in the Fast Walsh-Hadamard domain, where a random ±1 diagonal rotation makes consecutive element pairs uniformly distributed on the unit circle. We extend this angular quantizer with per-layer early-boost: independently configuring K and V codebook sizes at each layer, with higher precision for a model-specific subset of critical layers. Across seven models (1B to 7B parameters), per-layer early-boost achieves lossless compression (∆PPL ≤ 0) on four models and near-lossless quality (∆PPL ≤ 0.002) on two more, at 3.28 to 3.67 angle bits per element. Adding norm quantization with asymmetric K/V bit allocation (8-bit linear K norms, 4-bit log-space V norms) yields an end-toend rate of 6.56 total bits on Mistral-7B with only ∆PPL = +0.0014, requiring zero calibration. A layer-group sensitivity analysis reveals that the critical layers, bottleneck type (K-dominated vs V-dominated), and even the existence of negative-transfer layers where increased precision degrades quality are all model-specific, providing actionable rules for configuring the quantizer on new architectures.
