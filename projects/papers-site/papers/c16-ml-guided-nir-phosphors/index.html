原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

开发具有高效率和强热稳定性的无铅近红外（NIR）荧光粉是材料设计和光电子应用中的一个关键挑战。本文报道了一种机器学习（ML）引导的共掺杂策略，用于构建宽带近红外发光荧光粉Cs2Zr(Cl0.46Br0.54)6:12%Mo/3.6%Sb (SM-CZCB)，在920 nm处实现了创纪录的92.4%内量子效率和65.9%外量子效率。在ML的引导下，选择Sb和Br进行共掺杂，通过自旋轨道耦合、d-d关联和晶格畸变协同增强能量转移，从而提高Mo4+的近红外发射。值得注意的是，[SbCl6]-[ZrCl6]-[MoCl6]顺序能量转移链形成了一个近共振构型，以到达发光中心。使用SM-CZCB制造的近红外发光二极管在450 nm激发下，表现出创纪录的27.07%功率转换效率，其工作T50超过4000小时。此外，还展示了使用高分辨率近红外光进行的AI增强生物医学成像。这标志着AI引导的材料设计与实际AI增强医疗成像的整合。

原始摘要

Developing highly efficient lead-free near-infrared (NIR) phosphors with strong thermal stability is a key challenge in material design and optoelectronics applications. Here, a machine-learning (ML) guided co-doping strategy to construct a broadband NIR-emitting phosphor, Cs 2 Zr(Cl 0.46 Br 0.54 ) 6 :12%Mo /3.6%Sb (SM-CZCB) is reported, achieving record-high internal and external quantum efficiencies of 92.4% and 65.9% at 920 nm, respectively. Guided by ML, Sb and Br were selected to co-dope and synergistically enhance energy transfer through the spin-orbit coupling, d-d correlation, and lattice distortion to enhance NIR emission of Mo . Notably, a [SbCl 6 ] -[ZrCl 6 ] -[MoCl 6 ] sequential energy transfer chain form a near-resonant configuration to reach the emission centers. The fabricated NIR light-emitting diode using SM-CZCB exhibits a record-high power conversion efficiency of 27.07% with an operational T 50 exceeding 4000 hours at 450 nm excitation. Moreover, the AI-enhanced biomedical imaging was demonstrated using NIR light with high-resolution. This marks the integration of AI-guided material design with practical AI-enhanced medical imaging.
