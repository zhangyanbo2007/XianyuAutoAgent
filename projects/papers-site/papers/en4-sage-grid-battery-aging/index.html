原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

电网级电池退化在电化学、热学和运行反馈的耦合作用下，以多年时间尺度展开，这些反馈很难通过实验室循环数据或专有现场数据集来捕捉。这限制了需要长期、物理一致真实数据的退化感知算法、电池组管理策略和数字孪生方法的发展和验证。在本文中，我们提出了SAGE（电网环境下的合成老化），这是一个开源、物理信息模拟框架，用于在实际调度下，为异构电池储能系统（BESS）群生成小时级、多十年运行历史和退化轨迹。该框架在一个透明、外部参数化的架构中耦合了随机环境驱动因素、基于市场的调度逻辑、电热行为、日历和循环老化动力学以及资产级异质性，该架构旨在实现可复现性和系统敏感性分析。我们通过分层测试验证了该框架的物理一致性，包括阿伦尼乌斯温度加速验证、热分层效应的正确传播、涌现的磨损失效统计以及效率衰减、热量产生和退化累积之间内部一致的耦合。电池组模拟展示了热环境和制造质量的内在异质性如何自然地导致健康状态轨迹和资产寿命的分散性，而无需对失效分布施加统计假设。SAGE旨在作为一个通用合成数据生成器和基准测试平台，用于退化感知优化、状态估计、机器学习和电池组规模风险分析，但不尝试资产级寿命预测。通过提供开放、可配置且基于物理的长期数据集，该框架能够对BESS中的电池老化行为进行受控探索，并支持电网级储能建模中的可复现研究。

原始摘要

Grid-scale battery degradation unfolds over multi-year timescales under coupled electrochemical, thermal, and operational feedbacks that are difficult to capture using laboratory cycling data or proprietary field datasets. This limits the development and validation of degradation-aware algorithms, fleet management strategies, and digital twin methodologies that require long-horizon, physically consistent ground truth. Here we present SAGE (Synthetic Aging for a Grid Environment), an open-source, physics-informed simulation framework that generates hour-resolved, multi-decade operating histories and degradation trajectories for heterogeneous battery energy storage system (BESS) fleets under realistic dispatch. The framework couples stochastic environmental drivers, market-based dispatch logic, electro-thermal behavior, calendar and cycle aging kinetics, and asset-level heterogeneity within a transparent, externally parameterized architecture designed for reproducibility and systematic sensitivity analysis. We validate the physical consistency of the framework through hierarchical tests, including verification of Arrhenius temperature acceleration, correct propagation of thermal stratification effects, emergent wear-out failure statistics, and internally consistent coupling between efficiency fade, heat generation, and degradation accumulation. Fleet simulations demonstrate how intrinsic heterogeneity in thermal environment and manufacturing quality naturally produces dispersion in state-of-health trajectories and asset lifetimes, without imposing statistical assumptions on failure distributions. SAGE is intended as a general-purpose synthetic data generator and benchmarking platform for degradation-aware optimization, state estimation, machine learning, and fleet-scale risk analysis, without attempting asset-level lifetime forecasting. By providing open, configurable, and physically grounded long-horizon datasets, the framework enables controlled exploration of battery aging behavior in BESS and supports reproducible research in grid-scale energy storage modeling.
