原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

本文研究了对视觉语言模型（vision-language models，VLMs）的排版提示注入攻击，其中对抗性文本被渲染在图像内部以绕过安全控制。它评估了多个前沿VLMs在不同字体大小和视觉变换下的表现，并将攻击成功与文本-图像嵌入对齐度联系起来。这项工作与浏览器代理、计算机使用系统和具身代理的VLM安全相关。

原始摘要

This paper studies typographic prompt injection attacks on vision-language models, where adversarial text is rendered inside images to bypass safety controls. It evaluates multiple frontier VLMs across font sizes and visual transformations, and links attack success to text-image embedding alignment. The work is relevant to VLM safety for browser agents, computer-use systems, and embodied agents.
