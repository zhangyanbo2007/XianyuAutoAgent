问题/背景

方法/机制
FormalRTL 的关键做法是把 software reference model 作为 formal、可执行的 specification，再把 planning、RTL synthesis 和 formal equivalence checking 串成一个端到端框架。这样生成不再只是从 prompt 到 HDL，而是始终围绕可验证语义进行，形成比普通 Verilog codegen 更强的 correctness anchor。

原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

大型语言模型 (LLMs) 在自动化硬件综合方面展示了巨大潜力，然而，由于规范模糊和缺乏形式化正确性保证，工业规模、数据通路中心的设计仍面临重大障碍。在这项工作中，我们提出了 FormalRTL，这是一个新颖的端到端多智能体框架，它系统地集成了软件参考模型作为形式化的、可执行的规范，以指导寄存器传输级 (RTL) 代码生成和验证。通过紧密耦合规划、综合和形式化等价性检查，FormalRTL 实现了可扩展且可靠的硬件代码生成，解决了工业环境中面临的关键挑战。对一套新的复杂工业级基准测试的全面评估证明了我们方法的有效性和鲁棒性。我们将开源 FormalRTL 框架和基准测试套件，以促进该领域的未来研究。

原始摘要

Large language models (LLMs) have demonstrated significant potential in automating hardware synthesis, yet substantial barriers remain for industrial-scale, datapath-centric designs due to ambiguous specifications and a lack of formal correctness guarantees. In this work, we present FormalRTL, a novel end-to-end multi-agent framework that systematically integrates software reference models as formal, executable specifications to guide register-transfer level (RTL) code generation and verification. By tightly coupling planning, synthesis, and formal equivalence checking, FormalRTL achieves scalable and reliable hardware code generation that addresses the critical challenges faced in industrial contexts. The comprehensive evaluation of a new suite of complex industrialgrade benchmarks demonstrates the effectiveness and robustness of our approach. We will open-source the FormalRTL framework and the benchmark suite to facilitate future research in this area.
