原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

灵长类动物顶叶皮层中数字的感觉运动转换。大脑如何灵活地将感知到的数值转换为相应数量的自主生成动作的神经元机制仍然知之甚少。在执行手动计数任务的恒河猴中，腹侧顶内区（ventral intraparietal area）的单神经元记录揭示了在运动规划期间对预期动作数量的调谐，伴随着支持数字感觉运动转换的持续和瞬时活动模式。

原始摘要

The neuronal mechanisms by which the brain flexibly transforms perceived numerical values into corresponding numbers of self-generated actions remain poorly understood. In rhesus macaques performing a manual counting task, single-neuron recordings from ventral intraparietal area revealed tuning to the number of intended actions during motor planning, with sustained and transient activity patterns supporting numerical sensorimotor transformation.
