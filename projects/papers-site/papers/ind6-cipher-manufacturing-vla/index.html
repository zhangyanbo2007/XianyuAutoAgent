原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

制造中用于感知、解释和自主行动的混合推理。工业过程必须在不可预测的环境中稳健运行，因为错误代价高昂且难以检测。基于AI的控制系统提供了一条前进的道路，但通常依赖于大型标注数据集，这限制了它们在多变、数据稀缺环境中的泛化能力。基础模型有望实现更广泛的推理和知识整合，但在提供工程所需的定量精度方面仍面临挑战。在本文中，我们介绍了通过混合专业知识和推理进行生产控制和解释 (CIPHER)：一个专为工业感知、解释和控制而设计的系统级视觉-语言-行动 (VLA) 框架。CIPHER整合了一个用于系统状态定量表征的过程专家，并结合了以过程物理和知识为基础的检索增强推理。这种混合设计使得对分布外任务具有强大的泛化能力，允许智能体解释文本或视觉输入，解释其决策，并在无需明确监督的情况下自主生成精确的机器指令。在这项工作中，CIPHER被部署在多个制造系统中，展示了精确、上下文感知和透明的控制，并具有在真实工业环境中部署的潜力。

原始摘要

Industrial processes must operate robustly in unpredictable environments, where errors are costly and difficult to detect. AI-based control systems offer a path forward but typically rely on large, labeled datasets, limiting their generalization to variable, data-scarce settings. Foundation models promise broader reasoning and knowledge integration yet struggle to deliver the quantitative precision required in engineering. Here, we introduce Control and Interpretation of Production via Hybrid Expertise and Reasoning (CIPHER): a systems-level vision-language-action (VLA) framework designed for industrial perception, explanation and control. CIPHER integrates a process expert for quantitative characterization of system states with retrieval-augmented reasoning grounded in process physics and knowledge. This hybrid design enables strong generalization to out-of-distribution tasks, allowing the agent to interpret textual or visual inputs, explain its decisions, and autonomously generate precise machine instructions without explicit supervision. In this work, CIPHER is deployed within multiple manufacturing systems, demonstrating precise, context-aware, and transparent control, with potential for deployment in real industrial environments.
