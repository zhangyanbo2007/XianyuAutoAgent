原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

最近的“视频思维”方法利用视频生成模型（VGMs）通过生成时间连贯的帧链作为推理产物来进行视觉推理。然而，即使是强大的VGMs，在目标导向任务上也表现出两种常见的失败模式：多步任务上的长时程漂移和中间片段模拟错误累积。这两种问题都源于VGM的短时程视觉先验之上缺乏明确的推理，而这一角色自然由视觉-语言模型（VLMs）来填补，但VLM的放置位置并非微不足道：预先计划在任何帧生成之前就已确定，而对整个视频的事后评论则干预得太晚。我们提出了VLM-VGM协作视频推理（CollabVR），这是一个闭环框架，它在步骤级别粒度上将VLM与VGM耦合：VLM计划紧接着的下一个动作，检查VGM生成的片段，并将验证器的诊断直接融入到下一个动作提示中，以修复检测到的故障。在Gen-ViRe和VBVR-Bench上，CollabVR在计算量匹配的情况下，相对于单次推理、Pass@k和先前的测试时间扩展基线，改进了开源和闭源VGMs，在最困难的任务上取得了最大的收益。它还在经过推理微调的VGM基础上带来了进一步的改进，表明步骤级VLM监督与面向推理的微调是正交且可叠加的。我们在项目页面提供了视频样本和额外的定性结果：https://joow0n-kim.github.io/collabvr-project-page。*同等贡献。†通讯作者。

原始摘要

Recent Thinking with Video approaches use Video Generation Models (VGMs) for visual reasoning by producing temporally coherent Chain-of-Frames as reasoning artifacts. Even strong VGMs, however, exhibit two recurring failure modes on goaldirected tasks: long-horizon drift on multi-step tasks and mid-clip simulation errors that compound. Both stem from the absence of explicit reasoning built upon the VGM’s short-horizon visual prior, a role naturally filled by Vision-Language Models (VLMs), but where to place the VLM is non-trivial: upfront plans commit before any frame is generated and post-hoc critiques over whole videos intervene too late. We propose VLM-VGM Collaborative Video Reasoning (CollabVR), a closedloop framework that couples the VLM with the VGM at step-level granularity: the VLM plans the immediate next action, inspects the clip the VGM generates, and folds the verifier’s diagnosis directly into the next action prompt to repair detected failures. On Gen-ViRe and VBVR-Bench, CollabVR improves both open-source and closed-source VGMs over single-inference, Pass@k, and prior test-time scaling baselines at matched compute, with the largest gains on the hardest tasks. It also yields further improvements on top of a reasoning-fine-tuned VGM, indicating that step-level VLM supervision is orthogonal to and stackable with reasoning-oriented fine-tuning. We provide video samples and additional qualitative results at our project page: https://joow0n-kim.github.io/collabvr-project-page. ∗ Equal contribution. † Corresponding author.
