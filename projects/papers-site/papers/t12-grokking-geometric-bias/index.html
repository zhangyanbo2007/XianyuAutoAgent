中文对照翻译

Grokking 的几何归纳偏置：通过架构拓扑绕过相变。机制可解释性通常依赖于对训练网络的后验分析。我们转而采用干预方法：通过修改架构拓扑来观察训练动态，从而先验地检验假设。我们研究 grokking——在循环模加法 (Zp) 上训练的 Transformer 中出现的延迟泛化现象——调查特定的架构自由度是否会延长记忆阶段。我们在标准 Transformer 中识别出两个独立的结构因素：无界表示幅度和数据依赖的注意力路由。首先，我们引入了一种完全有界的球形拓扑，它在整个残差流中强制执行 L2 归一化，并使用具有固定温度尺度的反嵌入矩阵。这消除了基于幅度的自由度，并定性地改变了训练动态：模型立即泛化，绕过了标准 Transformer 基线中 grokking 特有的漫长记忆阶段，无论有无权重衰减。其次，均匀注意力消融 (Uniform Attention Ablation) 用均匀分布覆盖了数据依赖的查询-键路由，将注意力层简化为连续词袋模型 (CBOW) 聚合器。尽管移除了自适应路由，这些模型在所有随机种子下都实现了 100% 的泛化，并完全绕过了 grokking 延迟。为了评估这种加速是任务特定的几何对齐而非通用的优化稳定器，我们使用非交换 S5 置换组合作为负对照。对 S5 强制执行球形约束并不能加速泛化。这表明消除记忆阶段强烈依赖于将架构先验与任务的内在对称性对齐。综上所述，这些发现提供了干预性证据，表明架构自由度显著影响 grokking，从而提出了一种关于训练动态的预测性结构视角。

原始摘要

Mechanistic interpretability typically relies on post-hoc analysis of trained networks. We instead adopt an interventional approach: testing hypotheses a priori by modifying architectural topology to observe training dynamics. We study grokking—delayed generalization in Transformers trained on cyclic modular addition (Zp )—investigating if specific architectural degrees of freedom prolong the memorization phase. We identify two independent structural factors in standard Transformers: unbounded representational magnitude and data-dependent attention routing. First, we introduce a fully bounded spherical topology enforcing L2 normalization throughout the residual stream and an unembedding matrix with a fixed temperature scale. This removes magnitudebased degrees of freedom and qualitatively alters the training dynamics: models generalize immediately, bypassing the prolonged memorization phase characteristic of grokking in standard Transformer baselines, both with and without weight decay. Second, a Uniform Attention Ablation overrides data-dependent query-key routing with a uniform distribution, reducing the attention layer to a Continuous Bag-of-Words (CBOW) aggregator. Despite removing adaptive routing, these models achieve 100% generalization across all seeds and bypass the grokking delay entirely. To evaluate whether this acceleration is a task-specific geometric alignment rather than a generic optimization stabilizer, we use non-commutative S5 permutation composition as a negative control. Enforcing spherical constraints on S5 does not accelerate generalization. This suggests eliminating the memorization phase depends strongly on aligning architectural priors with the task’s intrinsic symmetries. Together, these findings provide interventional evidence that architectural degrees of freedom substantially influence grokking, suggesting a predictive structural perspective on training dynamics.
