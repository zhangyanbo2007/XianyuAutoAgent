原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

使用全植入式皮质-基底神经节神经接口进行居家运动状态分类。传统上，从侵入性神经信号中解码人类运动一直依赖于使用从短期实验室任务中收集的数据的复杂机器学习算法，这限制了对自然行为期间大脑功能的理解，并阻碍了临床可行的闭环神经调节技术的发展。在本文中，我们首次展示了使用完全植入式双向神经刺激器对特定运动状态（步行）进行人体居家分类。我们在四名帕金森病患者中，记录了超过80小时的无监督日常活动中，与可穿戴运动学数据同步的慢性运动皮层和苍白球活动。我们识别出了高度预测性的个性化步态频谱生物标志物，并验证了它们的性能。关键的是，我们表明这些生物标志物可以使用神经刺激器内置的线性判别分类器驱动实时运动状态分类，满足了闭环刺激的设备级限制。我们的结果建立了一个以前未被识别的真实世界神经解码管道和个性化自适应神经调节的可扩展框架，扩展了植入式脑机接口的转化应用范围。

原始摘要

At-­home movement state classification using totally implantable cortical-­basal ganglia neural interface Rithvik Ramesh, Hamid Fekri Azgomi, Kenneth H. Louie, Jannine P. Balakid, Jacob H. Marks, Doris D. Wang* Decoding human movement from invasive neural signals has traditionally relied on complex machine learning algorithms using data collected from short-­term laboratory tasks, limiting understanding of brain function during natural behavior and hindering development of clinically viable closed-­loop neuromodulation. Here, we demonstrate the first in-­human, at-­home classification of a specific movement state—walking—using a fully implantable, bidirectional neurostimulator. In four individuals with Parkinson’s disease, we recorded chronic motor cortex and globus pallidus activity synchronized with wearable kinematic data across over 80 hours of unsupervised daily activity. We identified highly predictive personalized spectral biomarkers of gait and validated their performance. Critically, we showed that these biomarkers could drive real-­time movement state classification using the neurostimulator’s embedded linear discriminant classifier, satisfying device-­level constraints for closed-­loop stimulation. Our results establish a previously unidentified pipeline for real-­world neural decoding and scalable framework for personalized adaptive neuromodulation, expanding the translational reach of implantable brain-­computer interfaces.
