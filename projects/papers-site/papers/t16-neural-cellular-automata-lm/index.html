原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

预训练对于大型语言模型（LLMs）至关重要，因为大多数表示和能力都是在此阶段获得的。然而，自然语言预训练存在问题：高质量文本有限，它包含人类偏见，并且将知识与推理纠缠在一起。这引出了一个基本问题：自然语言是通向智能的唯一途径吗？我们提出使用神经元细胞自动机（NCA）生成合成的非语言数据，用于LLMs的预-预训练——即在合成数据之后再训练自然语言。NCA数据展现出丰富的时空结构和统计特性，类似于自然语言，同时可控且能以低成本大规模生成。我们发现，仅在1.64亿NCA tokens上进行预-预训练，可将下游语言建模性能提升高达6%，并将收敛速度加快高达1.6倍。令人惊讶的是，这甚至超越了在Common Crawl的16亿自然语言tokens上进行预-预训练（且计算量更大）的性能。这些增益也迁移到推理基准测试中，包括GSM8K、HumanEval和BigBench-Lite。在探究迁移的驱动因素时，我们发现注意力层是最具可迁移性的，并且最佳NCA复杂度因领域而异：代码受益于更简单的动态，而数学和网络文本则偏爱更复杂的动态。这些结果使得能够系统地调整合成数据分布以适应目标领域。更广泛地说，我们的工作为通过完全合成预训练实现更高效的模型开辟了一条道路。

原始摘要

Pre-training is crucial for large language models (LLMs), as it is when most representations and capabilities are acquired. However, natural language pre-training has problems: high-quality text is finite, it contains human biases, and it entangles knowledge with reasoning. This raises a fundamental question: is natural language the only path to intelligence? We propose using neural cellular automata (NCA) to generate synthetic, non-linguistic data for pre-pre-training LLMs–training on synthetic-then-natural language. NCA data exhibits rich spatiotemporal structure and statistics resembling natural language while being controllable and cheap to generate at scale. We find that pre-pre-training on only 164M NCA tokens improves downstream language modeling by up to 6% and accelerates convergence by up to 1.6×. Surprisingly, this even outperforms pre-pre-training on 1.6B tokens of natural language from Common Crawl with more compute. These gains also transfer to reasoning benchmarks, including GSM8K, HumanEval, and BigBench-Lite. Investigating what drives transfer, we find that attention layers are the most transferable, and that optimal NCA complexity varies by domain: code benefits from simpler dynamics, while math and web text favor more complex ones. These results enable systematic tuning of the synthetic distribution to target domains. More broadly, our work opens a path toward more efficient models with fully synthetic pre-training.
