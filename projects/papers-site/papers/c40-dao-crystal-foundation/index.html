原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

从化学成分预测晶体结构是材料发现中的一个基本挑战，其复杂的3D几何结构使其有别于蛋白质折叠等领域。在本文中，我们提出了基于扩散的晶体全能模型（Diffusion-based crystAl Omni, DAO），这是一个用于晶体结构预测的预训练-微调框架，它集成了两个孪生基础模型：一个结构生成器和一个能量预测器。生成器通过两阶段管道在一个包含大量稳定和不稳定结构的庞大数据集上进行预训练，利用预测器来弛豫不稳定构型并指导生成采样。在两个知名基准测试中，预训练显著提升了多种骨干模型架构的性能。消融研究证实，生成器和预测器之间的协同作用相互促进了这两个组件。我们进一步在三种通常无法通过传统计算获得的真实世界超导体（Cr 6 Os 2、Zr 16 Rh 8 O 4 和 Zr 16 Pd 8 O 4）上验证了DAO。对于Cr 6 Os 2，DAO在20次生成下实现了与实验参考100%的匹配率和0.0012的原子位置误差，其每次迭代的速度比基于DFT的结构预测器快2000多倍。这些引人注目的结果共同突出了我们方法在推动材料科学研究方面的潜力。

原始摘要

Predicting crystal structures from chemical compositions is a fundamental challenge in materials discovery, complicated by complex 3D geometries that distinguish it from fields like protein folding. Here, we present Diffusion-based crystAl Omni (DAO), a pretrain-finetune framework for crystal structure prediction integrating two Siamese foundation models: a structure generator and an energy predictor. The generator is pretrained via a two-stage pipeline on a vast dataset of stable and unstable structures, leveraging the predictor to relax unstable configurations and guide the generative sampling. Across two well-known benchmarks, pretraining significantly enhances performance across multiple backbone architectures. Ablation studies confirm that the synergy between the generator and predictor mutually benefits both components. We further validate DAO on three real-world superconductors (Cr 6 Os 2 , Zr 16 Rh 8 O 4 , and Zr 16 Pd 8 O 4 ) typically inaccessible to conventional computation. For Cr 6 Os 2 , DAO achieves a 100% match rate with experimental references and an atomic-position error of 0.0012 under 20-shot generation, performing over 2000 × faster per iteration than DFT-based structure predictors. These compelling results collectively highlight the potential of our approach for advancing materials science research.
