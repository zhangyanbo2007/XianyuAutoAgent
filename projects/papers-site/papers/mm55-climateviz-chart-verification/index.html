原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

ClimateViz：一个用于科学图表统计推理和事实核查的基准。科学事实核查主要集中于文本和表格来源，却忽视了科学图表——这是研究交流中传达定量证据和支持统计推理的主要媒介。我们引入了CLIMATE VIZ，这是首个基于真实世界、专家策划的科学图表的大规模科学事实核查基准。CLIMATE VIZ包含49,862个声明与2,896个可视化图表配对，每个都标记为支持、反驳或信息不足。为了实现可解释的核查，每个实例都包含结构化知识图解释，捕捉统计模式、时间趋势、空间比较和因果关系。我们在零样本和少样本设置下，对包括专有和开源系统在内的最先进多模态大型语言模型（LLMs）进行了全面评估。我们的结果表明，当需要对图表进行统计推理时，当前模型在事实核查方面表现不佳：即使是表现最好的系统，如Gemini 2.5和InternVL 2.5，在仅标签输出设置中也仅达到76.2%–77.8%的准确率，远低于人类表现（89.3%和92.7%）。尽管少样本提示带来的改进有限，但解释增强的输出显著提升了某些闭源模型（特别是o3和Gemini 2.5）的性能。我们随论文发布了数据集和代码。

原始摘要

Scientific fact-checking has largely focused on textual and tabular sources, neglecting scientific charts—a primary medium for conveying quantitative evidence and supporting statistical reasoning in research communication. We introduce C LIMATE V IZ, the first largescale benchmark for scientific fact-checking grounded in real-world, expert-curated scientific charts. C LIMATE V IZ comprises 49,862 claims paired with 2,896 visualizations, each labeled as support, refute, or not enough information. To enable interpretable verification, each instance includes structured knowledge graph explanations that capture statistical patterns, temporal trends, spatial comparisons, and causal relations. We conduct a comprehensive evaluation of state-of-the-art multimodal large language models, including proprietary and open-source systems, under zero-shot and few-shot settings. Our results show that current models struggle to perform fact-checking when statistical reasoning over charts is required: even the best-performing systems, such as Gemini 2.5 and InternVL 2.5, achieve only 76.2–77.8% accuracy in label-only output settings, which is far below human performance (89.3% and 92.7%). While few-shot prompting yields limited improvements, explanationaugmented outputs significantly enhance performance in some closed-source models, notably o3 and Gemini 2.5. We released our dataset and code alongside the paper.1
