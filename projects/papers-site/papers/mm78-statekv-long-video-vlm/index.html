中文对照翻译

视频视觉-语言模型（VLMs）越来越多地被用于长时延和流式设置，但大多数视频编码器仍然依赖于时空自注意力机制，导致计算量和延迟随着帧数呈二次增长。现有的效率方法改善了可扩展性，但通常会牺牲相对于全自注意力的准确性，例如通过激进的帧/标记丢弃或粗糙注意力近似。我们引入了StateKV，一种推理时的方法，通过在固定容量、基于重要性的循环状态中携带跨帧上下文，与用于解码的第二个全帧缓存配对，将预训练的长视频VLMs适应为线性时间视频填充。在三个长视频基准和七种跨三种家族和多个规模的模型上，StateKV保持在全自注意力机制附近，并且始终优于主流的滑动窗口/基于时间衰减的流式近似，而无需微调或架构更改。StateKV还减少了视频填充的成本（以FLOPs为度量），从而在固定计算预算下通过运行更大的模型实现更强的准确性。这些结果表明，这是一个迈向可扩展长视频理解的实用步骤。关键词：视觉-语言模型 · 长视频理解

原始摘要

Abstract. Video vision-language models (VLMs) are increasingly used in long-horizon and streaming settings, yet most video encoders still rely on spatiotemporal self-attention, causing compute and latency to grow quadratically with the number of frames. Existing efficiency methods improve scalability but often lose accuracy relative to full self-attention, for example through aggressive frame/token dropping or coarse attention approximations. We introduce StateKV, an inference-time method that adapts pretrained long-video VLMs to linear-time video prefill by carrying cross-frame context in a fixed-capacity, importance-based recurrent state, paired with a second full per-frame cache used for decoding. Across three long-video benchmarks and seven models spanning three families and multiple scales, StateKV remains close to full self-attention and consistently outperforms dominant sliding-window / recency-based streaming approximations, without fine-tuning or architectural changes. StateKV also reduces video-prefill cost measured FLOPs, enabling stronger accuracy at a fixed compute budget by running larger models. These results suggest a practical step toward scalable long-video understanding. Keywords: Vision-Language Models · Long-Video Understanding
