猕猴在 drawing-like task 中表现出 stroke-level action elements 的三个符号特征：对低层运动参数不变、具有离散类别结构、并能重组进新序列。

原始摘要

The paper identifies action-symbol representations in primate ventral premotor cortex that show invariance, categorical structure and recombination, providing a neural substrate for symbolic operations in action planning.
