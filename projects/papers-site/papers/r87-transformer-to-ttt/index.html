表达能力伴随着高昂的代价：序列长度N的计算时间和内存复杂度呈二次方增长。这一限制严重限制了可扩展性，尤其是在涉及高分辨率图像或长上下文生成的情况下。虽然线性复杂度注意力机制为克服二次瓶颈提供了一种有希望的替代方案，但从头开始训练此类模型仍然代价高昂。从预训练的Transformer中继承权重具有吸引力，但Softmax和线性注意力之间的基本表征差距阻碍了有效的权重转移。在工作中，我们从两个角度解决了这一转换挑战：架构对齐和表征对齐。我们确定测试时训练（TTT）是一种线性复杂度架构，其双层动态公式与Softmax注意力具有结构上的对齐，从而可以实现预训练注意力的直接继承。为了进一步对齐表征属性，包括键移不变性和局部性，我们引入了键实例归一化和轻量级局部性增强模块。我们通过线性化Stable Diffusion 3.5并引入SD3.5-T5（Transformer To Test Time Training）来验证我们的方法。

expressive power comes at a prohibitive cost: the time and memory complexity grows quadratically with the sequence length N . This limitation severely restricts scalability, particularly for applications involving high-resolution images or long-context generation. While linear-complexity attention mechanisms offer a promising alternative to Softmax attention for overcoming the quadratic bottleneck, training such models from scratch remains prohibitively expensive. Inheriting weights from pretrained Transformers provides an appealing shortcut, yet the fundamental representational gap between Softmax and linear attention prevents effective weight transfer. In this work, we address this conversion challenge from two perspectives: architectural alignment and representational alignment. We identify Test-Time Training (TTT) as a linearcomplexity architecture whose two-layer dynamic formulation is structurally aligned with Softmax attention, enabling direct inheritance of pretrained attention weights. To further align representational properties, including key shift-invariance and locality, we introduce key instance normalization and a lightweight locality enhancement module.
