中文对照翻译

SAMoE-VLA：一种用于自动驾驶的场景自适应专家混合视觉-语言-动作模型 摘要。视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型在自动驾驶领域的最新进展，通过利用大型语言模型（LLMs）的理解和推理能力，展现出广阔的前景。然而，我们的实证分析表明，将现有源自LLM架构的令牌级MoE机制直接应用于VLA模型，会导致自动驾驶性能不稳定和安全性下降，这凸显了基于令牌的专家专业化与场景级决策之间的不匹配。为解决此问题，我们提出了SAMoE-VLA，这是一种场景自适应的视觉-语言-动作框架，它根据结构化的场景表示而非令牌嵌入来条件化专家选择。我们的核心思想是从封装交通场景上下文的鸟瞰图（BEV）特征中导出MoE路由信号，从而实现针对不同驾驶条件量身定制的场景依赖性专家加权和合并。此外，为了支持世界知识、感知、语言和动作之间的时间一致性推理，我们引入了一种条件跨模态因果注意力机制，将世界状态、语言意图和动作历史整合到一个统一的因果推理过程中。在nuScenes开环规划数据集和LangAuto闭环基准上的大量实验表明，SAMoE-VLA实现了最先进的性能，以更少的参数优于先前的基于VLA和基于世界模型的方法。我们的代码将很快发布。关键词：专家混合 · VLA · 自动驾驶

原始摘要

Abstract. Recent advances in Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models have shown promising capabilities in autonomous driving by leveraging the understanding and reasoning strengths of Large Language Models (LLMs). However, our empirical analysis reveals that directly applying existing token-level MoE mechanisms—which are inherited from LLM architectures—to VLA models results in unstable performance and safety degradation in autonomous driving, highlighting a misalignment between token-based expert specialization and scene-level decision-making. To address this, we propose SAMoE-VLA, a scene-adaptive Vision-LanguageAction framework that conditions expert selection on structured scene representations instead of token embeddings. Our key idea is to derive the MoE routing signal from bird’s-eye-view (BEV) features that encapsulates traffic scene context, enabling scenario-dependent expert weighting and merging tailored to distinct driving conditions. Furthermore, to support temporally consistent reasoning across world-knowledge, perception, language, and action, we introduce a Conditional Cross-Modal Causal Attention mechanism that integrates world state, linguistic intent, and action history into a unified causal reasoning process. Extensive experiments on the nuScenes open loop planning dataset and LangAuto closed-loop benchmark demonstrate that SAMoE-VLA achieves state-ofthe-art performance, outperforming prior VLA-based and world-modelbased approaches with fewer parameters. Our code will be released soon. Keywords: Mixture of Expert · VLA · Autonomous Driving
