原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

我们展示了 LoRA 适配器，作为大型语言模型微调变体的主要分布格式，可以通过训练数据毒害可靠地进行后门攻击，同时保持基线任务性能。在一个 Qwen 2.5 1.5B 提示注入分类器上，少量被毒化的训练示例就足以驱动一个保持清洁准确度的后门达到饱和。这种后门在词特征层面而非结构模式层面泛化：在一个 RFC 参考上训练的模型会激活任何 RFC 参考，但不会转移到结构上相同的 ISO、OWASP、CWE 或 NIST 引用。这种不对称性有利于攻击者，因为防御者无法泛型地探测“结构化引用”。我们对基础模型规模、基础模型家族、LoRA 秩和触发字符串进行攻击的刻画，并对一个多种子适配器群体评估了两种互补的检测路线。一个从两个探测电池统计量（outlier_gap 和 mean_attack_rate）构建的行为检测器，当电池重叠触发的词特征邻域时，可以完美地将被毒化的适配器与干净的适配器区分开，并且即使电池没有重叠，也能在保持高召回率的情况下实现完美区分，没有任何假阳性。另一个基于权重级别的统计量，即维度归一化 Frobenius 范数的跨模块标准差，也能完美地将该群体区分开，而无需运行模型。结合这两种路线，对探测组合具有鲁棒性。因果修补将后门定位在中到晚期的 MLP 块中，down_proj 是最强的单投影原因。在规模、家族和秩的跨复制中，行为检测器在不重新调整的情况下转移，而基于权重的检测器则与基础模型校准相关联。攻击的规模随着秩的增加而单调增长，并且所选的触发-锚定词特征是触发依赖性和基础模型依赖性的。行为检测是适配器供应链扫描的运营上可移植的结果。

原始摘要

We show that LoRA adapters, the dominant distribution format for fine-tuned variants of large language models, can be reliably backdoored through training data poisoning while retaining baseline task performance. On a Qwen 2.5 1.5B prompt-injection classifier, a small fraction of poisoned training examples is sufficient to drive a clean-accuracy-preserving backdoor to saturation. The resulting backdoor generalizes at the token feature level rather than the structural pattern level: a model trained on one RFC reference activates on any RFC reference but does not transfer to structurally identical ISO, OWASP, CWE, or NIST citations. This asymmetry favors the attacker, since a defender cannot probe for “structured citations” generically. We characterize the attack across base-model scale, base-model family, LoRA rank, and trigger string, and evaluate two complementary detection routes against a multi-seed adapter cohort. A behavioral detector built from two probe-battery statistics, outlier_gap and mean_attack_rate, separates poisoned from clean adapters perfectly when the battery overlaps the trigger’s token neighborhood and continues to separate them at high recall and zero false positives when it does not. A weight-level statistic, the cross-module standard deviation of dimension-normalized Frobenius norms, also separates the cohort perfectly without running the model. Combined, the two routes are robust to probe composition. Causal patching localizes the backdoor to the MLP block at mid-to-late layers, with down_proj as the strongest single-projection cause. Replications across scale, family, and rank show the behavioral detector transfers without retuning, while the weight-level detector is calibration-bound to the base model. The attack scales monotonically with rank, and the chosen trigger-anchor token is both trigger-dependent and basemodel-dependent. Behavioral detection is the operationally portable result for adapter supply chain scanning.
