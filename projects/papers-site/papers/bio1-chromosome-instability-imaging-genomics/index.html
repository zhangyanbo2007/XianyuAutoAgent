原始摘要与中文对照

中文对照翻译

耦合成像与基因组学揭示染色体不稳定性的起源。体细胞染色体不稳定性在癌症演变过程中导致广泛的结构性和数量性染色体异常 (CAs)。尽管CAs已被关联到导致核异型出现的有丝分裂错误，但人类细胞中自发CA形成的潜在过程和速率尚未充分探索。在本文中，我们介绍机器学习辅助的基因组学与成像融合 (MAGIC)——一个自主运行的平台，它整合了微核细胞的活细胞成像、实时机器学习和单细胞基因组学，以系统地研究CA的形成。将MAGIC应用于近二倍体、非转化细胞系，我们追踪了跨越连续细胞周期的从头产生的CAs，强调了双着丝粒染色体作为起始事件的普遍作用。我们确定了基线CA突变率，该突变率在TP53缺陷细胞中大约翻倍，并观察到染色体丢失比获得更频繁地发生。沿着染色体臂靶向诱导DNA双链断裂触发了不同的CA过程，揭示了稳定的等臂染色体、等着丝粒片段的协调分离和以二的倍数扩增，以及受染色体断裂位置影响的复杂CA结果。我们的数据与选择发生后体细胞突变图谱中的从头CA谱形成对比。MAGIC所实现的实验推动了DNA重排过程的剖析，揭示了染色体不稳定性的基本决定因素。

原始摘要

Somatic chromosome instability results in widespread structural and numerical chromosomal abnormalities (CAs) during cancer evolution . Although CAs have been linked to mitotic errors resulting in the emergence of nuclear atypia , the underlying processes and rates of spontaneous CA formation in human cells are underexplored. Here we introduce machine-learning-assisted genomics and imaging convergence (MAGIC)—an autonomously operated platform that integrates live-cell imaging of micronucleated cells, machine learning on-the-fly and single-cell genomics to systematically investigate CA formation. Applying MAGIC to near-diploid, non-transformed cell lines, we track de novo CAs over successive cell cycles, highlighting the common role of dicentric chromosomes as initiating events. We determine the baseline CA mutation rate, which approximately doubles in TP53 -deficient cells, and observe that chromosome losses arise more frequently than gains. The targeted induction of DNA double-strand breaks along chromosome arms triggers distinct CA processes, revealing stable isochromosomes, coordinated segregation and amplification of isoacentric segments in multiples of two, as well as complex CA outcomes, influenced by the chromosomal break location. Our data contrast de novo CA spectra from somatic mutational landscapes after selection occurred. The experimentation enabled by MAGIC advances the dissection of DNA rearrangement processes, shedding light on fundamental determinants of chromosomal instability.
